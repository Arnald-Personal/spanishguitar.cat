About me

Traditional Spanish Music for your Big Day

Barcelona is a city where tradition meets avant-garde, and your wedding music should reflect that same harmony. As a specialist in destination weddings in Barcelona, I provide a sophisticated instrumental soundtrack that fits perfectly within the city's most iconic settings—from the historic Gothic Quarter to the luxury terraces overlooking the Mediterranean.

Whether you are hosting an intimate ceremony in a boutique hotel or a grand celebration in a modernist palace, the Spanish guitar brings an authentic sense of place. My repertoire for Barcelona weddings focuses on elegant instrumental covers of modern hits and Spanish classics, designed to provide a chic atmosphere during your cocktail hour or a soul-stirring accompaniment to your 'I do' moment.

Curated Repertoire

A bespoke musical selection tailored to your taste. From timeless Spanish classical masterpieces to modern pop and rock anthems reimagined for the guitar, I work closely with you to create the perfect atmosphere for every moment of your celebration.

The Performance
