About me

Traditional Spanish Music for your Big Day

Imagine walking down the aisle to the warm, soul-stirring sound of a Spanish classical guitar. Whether you are planning an intimate ceremony in a Costa Brava cove, a luxury wedding in a Barcelona manor, or a romantic celebration in Sitges, I provide the authentic musical touch that your guests will remember forever.

I specialize in performing for international couples who choose the stunning landscapes of Catalonia for their destination wedding. My repertoire blends traditional Spanish pieces with contemporary arrangements, tailored to the unique atmosphere of your celebration.
