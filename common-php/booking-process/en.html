How it works in practice

Booking Process

We work with innovative methodologies to ensure that the entire reformatting process is done from start to finish as planned.

01

Enquire Online

After filling in your event details on our online contact form, you will receive a personalised quote from our team on the or next working day.

02

Call with Benja

We can arrange a call with Benja to discuss your entertainment for the day and make sure that all your musical requirements are taken care of.

03

Booking Form

A form is sent for you to confirm all the relevant information for your day as well as the agreed fees, after which an invoice will be sent.

04

Deposit

We then request a 50% non refundable deposit to secure your date. Your booking will be confirmed on receipt of the deposit.

05

Playlist

Once your booking is confirmed we will send a blank Spotify playlist for you to start adding any requests you would like incorporated into your event.

05

Final Payment

Your balance is due 1 month before your wedding. Any pre agreed travel or accommodation will have now been fully arranged and confirmed.

05

Showtime

The musician/s will arrive to sound check at least 1 hour prior to the agreed start time.
